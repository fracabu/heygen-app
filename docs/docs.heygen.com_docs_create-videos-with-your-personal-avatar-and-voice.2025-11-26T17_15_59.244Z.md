# Create Videos with Your Personal Avatar and Voice

Bring your personalized avatar to life with custom voice and video creation

With the HeyGen v2 API, you can create videos using both existing avatars and **Instant Avatars** that you generate yourself. In this tutorial, we’ll walk you through creating videos featuring your own Instant Avatar and custom voice.

## Generate An Instant Avatar on Web Interface   [Skip link to Generate An Instant Avatar on Web Interface](https://docs.heygen.com/docs/create-videos-with-your-personal-avatar-and-voice\#generate-an-instant-avatar-on-web-interface)

Begin by creating your unique **Instant Avatar** on the [Create Instant Avatar](https://app.heygen.com/avatars/create-instant-avatar) page.

During the final step of creation, you'll assign a _name_ to your avatar, with this _name_ you will choose your avatar and voice.

If you already have an Instant Avatar, you can find its _name_ on the [Avatars](https://app.heygen.com/avatars) page.

## Generate a Custom Voice on Web Interface   [Skip link to Generate a Custom Voice on Web Interface](https://docs.heygen.com/docs/create-videos-with-your-personal-avatar-and-voice\#generate-a-custom-voice-on-web-interface)

Create your unique custom voice on the [Voices](https://app.heygen.com/voices) page.

Click **Create New Voice**, and choose one of the following options:

- Instant Voice Cloning – Provide an audio file or recording to clone a voice.
- Generate Voice – Enter a name and a text prompt to generate a new voice.

Once created, your custom voice will be saved, and its `display_name` will appear in the list of voices.

## List Avatars   [Skip link to List Avatars](https://docs.heygen.com/docs/create-videos-with-your-personal-avatar-and-voice\#list-avatars)

The [List All Avatars (V2)](https://docs.heygen.com/reference/list-avatars-v2) API call will provide you with a list of avatars. Find the _name_ you gave your Instant Avatar here and get its `avatar_id`.

## List Voices   [Skip link to List Voices](https://docs.heygen.com/docs/create-videos-with-your-personal-avatar-and-voice\#list-voices)

The [List All Voices (V2)](https://docs.heygen.com/reference/list-voices-v2) API call will provide you with a list of voices. The `display_name` field will contain the name of your voice. You can find your voice name on the **Voice** page.

Assuming you've got your `avatar_id` and a `voice_id`, you're all set to move on to the next steps.

## Next Steps   [Skip link to Next Steps](https://docs.heygen.com/docs/create-videos-with-your-personal-avatar-and-voice\#next-steps)

You can proceed from this point by following the instructions on the [Create Avatar Videos](https://docs.heygen.com/docs/create-video#2-generate-video) page. The process remains the same - just ensure that you update the `avatar_id` and `voice_id` fields with the values you've obtained.
